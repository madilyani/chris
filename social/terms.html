Definitions

  • Funding will allow Gaggle Studios to improve the speed and quality of their development pipeline significantly. Based in LA and Toronto, Gaggle Studios currently has several titles hosted on their platform at www.gaggle.fun. The flagship title, Goose Goose Duck, is a voice-chat integrated social deception game featuring ducks.   • Goose Goose Duck is a voice-chat integrated social deception game where players must work together to maintain their spaceship, the SS Mother Goose, during their interplanetary quest while also avoiding malicious Mallards who are out to get them.
  • Gaggle Studios, Inc, was founded in April 2020 by four members: CTO Simon Chu, CEO Shawn Fischtein, Technical Lead Christopher Gloyd, and Art Director Ben Gallego.
