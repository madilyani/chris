Who are we?

We’re a team of industry veterans in the gaming and entertainment space, with fun loving social team members whose talents range from creating spectacular drawings on the fly, to figuring out difficult coding problems.

Why did we create Gaggle.fun

We created Gaggle when we saw there was an increased need for people to stay connected. We looked around and noticed there was a huge lack of games that made it easy to play together right away, without having to set up different programs and apps.

What makes us special?

We're the first of our kind to fully integrate voice chat into our gaming platform - making it that much easier to play with your grandma, family, and friends.
